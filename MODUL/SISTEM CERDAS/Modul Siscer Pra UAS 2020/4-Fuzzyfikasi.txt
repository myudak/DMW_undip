Linear naik
x<= a -->  0
a<=x<=b  --> (x-a)/(b-a)
x>=b --> 1
-----------------------------
Linear turun
x= a --> 1
a<=x<=b --> (b-x)/(b-a)
x>=b --> 0
--------------------------------
Segitiga kurva
x<=a atau x>=c --> 0
a<=x<=b   -> (x-a)/(b-a)  ----> naik
b<=x<=c  --> (c-x)/(c-b) -----> turun
x=b ---> 1  ---> puncak
--------------------------------
